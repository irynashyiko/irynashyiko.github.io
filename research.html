Stage 6

Research foundation for a practical artist tool.

This project combines music business context, UX thinking, and prototype development to improve financial transparency.

Aim

Investigate how a digital tool can improve payment transparency, tracking, and revenue visibility for independent artists and labels.

Research question

How can digital tools enhance financial transparency and revenue understanding in the independent music ecosystem?

Methods

Mixed approach combining:

  • Artist and label interviews
  • Surveys on payment tracking behavior
  • Case studies of existing tools
  • Practice-based UX concept development

Expected impact

Support artists with clearer payment visibility, stronger decision-making, and a more sustainable financial workflow.

Professional relevance

Research to real-world artist support.

  • Bridges research, UX, and industry practice
  • Supports artist management and business strategy
  • Focuses on sustainable creative careers
