Stage 3

The money exists. The visibility does not.

Independent artists are paid from multiple systems that rarely align. This creates stress, confusion, and weak financial control.

Fragmented statements

Streaming, publishing, and performance royalties usually appear in separate places with separate formats.

Unclear payment timing

Even when income is correct, timelines differ, making it hard to know what is pending versus what is missing.

Harder decisions

Without a full picture, artists and managers make release, budget, and growth decisions with partial information.

Real-world friction

Too many tabs. Not enough clarity.

  • Different labels for similar revenue categories
  • Different reporting periods and payout cycles
  • No single place to reconcile payment status

What should the tool solve first?

Visibility before complexity.
