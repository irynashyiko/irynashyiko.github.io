The problem

Artists don’t have a clear view of their income.

Music income is split across streaming platforms, PRS, PPL, live payments, and invoices. These systems are fragmented, delayed, and difficult to interpret, making it hard for artists to see what they have earned and what still needs attention.

Why is this important?

Artists need one clear place to understand where their money comes from.

Music income is usually split across streaming platforms, PRS and PPL statements, direct payments, and client invoices. This project brings those pieces into one structured dashboard so artists can connect accounts, log income, track what is paid or still pending, and create invoices without losing visibility.

Future direction

Exploring Open Banking integration

Future development of this project may explore Open Banking to support faster payments, clearer transaction tracking, and consent-based connections between financial systems.

Prototype scope

This is an early-stage concept demonstration.

This prototype shows how artist income could be structured in one system. It is not a fully connected product.

  • Payments are manually logged for demonstration purposes
  • Platform integrations are simulated
  • No real financial data is processed
  • Future development may include Open Banking integration
